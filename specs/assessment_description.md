# Assessment Purpose
Congratulations on passing phase one of the MSA program!
To pass Phase 2, you must complete either the software stream or data science stream. This assessment is
for the software stream. This is an individual assessment. You will be ranked against your peers.
Building on our foundational knowledge of React and REST APIs using the .NET framework from Phase 1,
this assessment will challenge you to develop a full stack application with advanced features. This project
aims to demonstrate your proficiency in these technologies, as well as your ability to independently research
and integrate new technologies.
## Task
In this assessment you are to develop a full stack web application. The application must be based on the
theme mentioned in the following section.
All the basic requirements are required. Additionally you are to achieve at minimum three advanced
requirements.
## Theme - Gamification
The theme for this year's assignment is Gamification. In Human-Computer Interaction (HCI), gamification
is the practice of integrating game design elements—such as points, badges, achievements, streaks,
leaderboards, and progress tracking—into non-game applications to improve user engagement, motivation,
and overall user experience. This does not mean you have to create a game.
One example could be a study app that rewards users for completing tasks and maintaining learning streaks.
Students are encouraged to apply HCI and gamification principles creatively in their software solution.
Originality and innovation will be valued.

## AI Usage
Artificial Intelligence (AI) tools such as GitHub Copilot, ChatGPT, Microsoft Copilot, Claude, and similar
technologies may be used throughout this assessment.
AI is becoming an essential part of modern software development, and students are encouraged to use it to
support research, planning, coding, testing, debugging, and documentation. However, students are expected
to critically evaluate AI-generated outputs and demonstrate an understanding of the solutions they
implement.
As part of your submission, you must briefly describe how AI was used during the development process.
Effective and responsible use of AI will form part of the overall assessment. (For more details refer to the
submission section)
